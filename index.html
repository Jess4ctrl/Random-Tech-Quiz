Random Tech Quiz

Welcome

Test your knowledge in computers and technology. Choose a difficulty and start a random quiz powered by OpenTDB.

How it works
  • 10 multiple-choice questions from OpenTDB (Computer category).
  • Choose a difficulty: Any, Easy, Medium, or Hard.
  • Click an answer once to lock it in. Answers reveal immediately.
  • Use the Next button to go to the following question.
  • Your score shows at the end, and you can retry.

About

This website was made to help users test their knowledge about computers and technology. Each quiz gives 10 random questions that cover different topics like hardware, software, and general tech facts.

You can choose your preferred difficulty level and see how much you really know. Whether you’re just learning or already a tech lover, this quiz is a quick and fun way to challenge yourself.
